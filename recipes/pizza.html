Pizza Recipe

Description

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon of yeast
  • Teaspoon of salt
  • 3 cups of bread flour
  • Teaspoon of oil
  • 1 cup of water

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven – Set your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Prepare Dough – Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Add Sauce – Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add Toppings – Sprinkle cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake – Place in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes until golden.
  6. Enjoy – Let it cool slightly, then slice and enjoy!
